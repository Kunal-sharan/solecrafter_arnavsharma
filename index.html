HOW IT'S DONE
After you have finalised your design begins the manufacturing process by sourcing the necessary materials and creating a physical prototype. It is then processed through our printing machines.
Our designers then hand make the illustrations and check for printing errors.
The custom shoe is then shipped to the customer, who can enjoy a one-of-a-kind pair of shoes designed to their exact specifications.
